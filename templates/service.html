What services does Ajay Engineering Construction Limited offer?
1-Pumping: Using specialized pumps to remove water from the site. These pumps can vary in size and capacity depending on the volume of water that needs to be removed and the depth of the excavation. 2-Wellpoint Systems: Installing wellpoint systems, which consist of closely spaced shallow wells connected to a common header pipe. These systems are effective in lowering the groundwater table in the vicinity of the excavation. 3-Deep Well Systems: Installing deep well dewatering systems for sites with deeper groundwater levels. Deep well systems use pumps to extract water from deep aquifers. 4-Sump Pump Installation: Installing sump pumps in excavations or basements to collect and remove water that accumulates in low-lying areas. 5- Monitoring and Maintenance: Monitoring groundwater levels and pump performance to ensure effective dewatering throughout the duration of the project. This may involve adjusting pump rates or repositioning equipment as needed. 6- Water Treatment: Depending on local regulations and environmental concerns, dewatering services may also include water treatment to remove contaminants before discharge.
